Arrhythmia is a condition where the heart beats irregularly.

Symptoms of arrhythmia include:
- palpitations
- dizziness
- chest pain
- shortness of breath

Common types of arrhythmia:
- Atrial fibrillation
- Ventricular tachycardia
- Bradycardia

Arrhythmia can be detected using ECG signals.

Treatment may include medication, pacemakers, or lifestyle changes.
